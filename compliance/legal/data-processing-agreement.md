# Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
## Template for Customer Organizations

### BETWEEN:
**Controller:** [Customer Organization Name] ("Controller")
**Processor:** [Company Name], CUI [CUI] ("Processor")

### 1. Subject Matter and Duration
This DPA governs the processing of personal data by the Processor on behalf of the Controller through the EU Funds Platform, for the duration of the service agreement.

### 2. Nature and Purpose of Processing
- Management of EU funding proposals
- Organization eligibility verification
- AI-assisted document generation
- Qualified Electronic Signature facilitation

### 3. Categories of Data Subjects
- Controller's employees and representatives
- Authorized signatories
- Contact persons for funding proposals

### 4. Categories of Personal Data
- Identity data (name, position, CNP for QES)
- Contact data (email, phone)
- Professional data (role, qualifications)
- Organization data (CUI, fiscal information)

### 5. Processor Obligations

5.1. Process personal data only on documented instructions from the Controller.
5.2. Ensure persons authorized to process have committed to confidentiality.
5.3. Implement technical and organizational security measures per Article 32.
5.4. Not engage sub-processors without prior written authorization.
5.5. Assist the Controller with data subject rights requests.
5.6. Delete or return all personal data upon termination.
5.7. Make available all information necessary for audits.

### 6. Sub-processors

| Sub-processor | Purpose | Location |
|---------------|---------|----------|
| AWS (Amazon) | Cloud infrastructure | EU (London) |
| certSIGN | QES signatures | Romania |
| OpenAI | AI text generation (no PII) | EU endpoint |

### 7. International Transfers
All processing within EU/EEA. Standard Contractual Clauses applied where necessary.

### 8. Security Measures
- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Role-based access control
- Multi-factor authentication
- Comprehensive audit logging
- Automated vulnerability scanning
- Incident response within 72 hours

### 9. Breach Notification
Processor shall notify Controller without undue delay (within 24 hours) after becoming aware of a personal data breach.

### 10. Audit Rights
Controller may audit Processor's compliance with this DPA once per year with 30 days written notice.

### 11. Governing Law
This DPA is governed by Romanian law and GDPR.
